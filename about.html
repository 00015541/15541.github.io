About us

The first of 19 French coffee shops Bon! Here you can start the day with a healthy breakfast, continue it with a business lunch with colleagues, or in the evening, meet your loved ones over a cup of coffee with a delicate dessert with French motifs. You can take any products, including coffee, with you.

Our highly qualified European experts train local staff in the traditions of our cuisine and ensure that all aspects of our service and surrounds are authentic. We have a delicious range of traditional French recipes and a menu that encompasses: breads, sandwiches, pancakes, waffles, ice cream, all accompanied by the finest teas, coffees or fresh juices. Come and visit us and sample our chic décor and take-away the finest French fancies in beautifully packaged parcels – c’est trés, trés Bon!

History

In 2009, the first French coffee shop, Bon!, opened on Chekhov street. It required an investment of about $150 thousand to launch. It started as a small coffee shop, and we reinvested all the income back into the business. As we grew, we had the opportunity to expand and invested a significant amount of money in importing equipment from Italy.

Over the years, Bon! has gained a reputation for not only its excellent coffee but also its cozy and welcoming atmosphere. We take pride in creating a space where customers can relax and enjoy their time with friends and family.
